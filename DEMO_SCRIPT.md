# 🎯 Hackathon Demo Script - Trending Token Tracker

## 🎬 **Opening (30 seconds)**
*"Welcome to Trending Token Tracker - the gasless crypto trading app that makes crypto simple for beginners!"*

## 📊 **Problem Statement (30 seconds)**
*"Crypto beginners struggle with two main issues:*
1. *They don't know which tokens are trending*
2. *Even when they find tokens, buying requires gas fees and complex steps*

*Our solution? A one-stop app that shows trending tokens and lets users buy instantly without gas fees."*

### **Step 1: Show the Interface**
*"As you can see, we have a beautiful, professional interface with:*
- *Real-time trending tokens from CoinDCX API*
- *Live price data, volume, and market cap*
- *Professional stats dashboard*
- *Transaction history tracking"*

### **Step 2: Demonstrate Token Discovery**
*"Here are the top 3 trending tokens right now. Notice the real-time data:*
- *Price changes in real-time*
- *Volume and market cap information*
- *Beautiful, responsive design"*

### **Step 3: Show Gasless Trading**
*"Now, let me demonstrate the one-click gasless buying experience:*
1. *I'll select a token - let's buy $10 of [TOKEN]*
2. *Click the buy button*
3. *Watch the gasless swap execute*
4. *See the success confirmation*
5. *Check the transaction history - notice it shows 'Gasless' with 0 gas used"*

### **Step 4: Highlight Key Features**

- ✅ *Real CoinDCX API integration*
